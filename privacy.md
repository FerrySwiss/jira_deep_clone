# Privacy Policy - Deep Clone for Jira

This Privacy Policy describes how your data is handled by the **Deep Clone for Jira** app.

## 1. Data Collection and Storage
This app is built using the **Atlassian Forge** platform. 
*   **No External Storage**: This app does NOT store your Jira data on any external servers. 
*   **Forge Infrastructure**: All data processing (including the cloning of issues and attachments) is done within the Atlassian-managed infrastructure.
*   **No Tracking**: We do not use any tracking or analytics cookies.

## 2. Data Usage
The app only accesses your Jira data (issues, comments, attachments, and links) for the sole purpose of performing the "Clone" operation as requested by the user. 
*   **Cloning**: Data is read from the source issue and written to the new target issue.
*   **Attachment Handling**: The app processes attachments up to **20MB** in size to perform the cloning operation. Files larger than this are skipped for performance reasons.
*   **Permissions**: The app operates under the permissions granted to the Forge system and the individual user's authorization.

## 3. Data Sharing
*   **No Third-Party Sharing**: Your data is never shared with any third-party services or stored outside of your Atlassian site.

## 4. Contact
For any questions regarding this app's privacy practices, please contact the internal IT / Automation team at FerrySwiss.
